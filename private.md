# Privacy Policy for Buddee Grow Assistant

Effective Date: 09/12/2024

Buddee Grow Assistant is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we handle the information you provide when using our app.

## Information We Collect
We do not collect any personal data or identifiable information about you. The only information stored in the app is the data you enter regarding your grows.

## Data Storage and Control
All grow-related data is stored locally on your device. This means:
- **You have full control over your data.** 
- You can remove all stored data at any time through the app's **Settings** menu by choosing to reset or restart.

## Data Sharing
We do not collect, store, or share your data with any third parties. All information remains private and accessible only on your device.

## Ads and In-App Purchases
Buddee Grow Assistant includes advertisements and optional in-app purchases. These services may collect limited data to provide targeted advertising or process transactions. Please refer to the privacy policies of the respective service providers for more information:
- [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

## Your Privacy Rights
As no data is collected or stored on our servers, your grow information is entirely within your control. Should you choose to reset the app, all data will be permanently erased from your device.

## Updates to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.
